Recipe

Chocolate cake

"This chocolate cake is a classic. This version of the recipe is fast and quick and your kids will LOVE IT!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and flour

3

Mix everything together to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in owen
